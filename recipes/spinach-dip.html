The best spinach artichoke dip

Description

This is our best version of the classic party dip. It's savory and creamy with a slight tang from sour cream and Parmesan -- so hard to resist.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz frozen chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup minced shallot
  • 2 green onions
  • 14 oz can quartered artichokes
  • 1 1/2 cups greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup shredded parmaesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • paprika
  • pita chips for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Thaw the spinach. Then use a strainer to squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
  3. Mince the shallot. Thinly slice the green onions. Roughly chop the artichokes.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together the spinach, shallot, green onions, and artichokes with the greek yogurt, mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, dried dill, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Taste and add a gew pinches of kosher salt if desired.
  5. Spread the dip in an even layer in a small baking dish. Top with a sprinkle of shredded parmesan (and if desired, a sprinkle of paprika).
  6. Bake for 20 minutes until warmed through and the cheese is melted
  7. Serve warm with pita chips.

Note: To make ahead info:

Make up to 1 day in advance and refrigerate in a bowl. Transfer to a baking dish for baking: you may need to bake a few extra minutes until fully warmed through.
